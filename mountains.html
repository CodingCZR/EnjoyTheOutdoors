Types of Mountains

Mountains can be classified into different types based on their formation and characteristics. Here are some of the common types of mountains:

  • Fold Mountains
  • Fault-Block Mountains
  • Volcanic Mountains
  • Dome Mountains
  • Plateau Mountains

Each type of mountain has its own unique features and geological processes that contribute to its formation. Exploring these different types of mountains can provide a deeper understanding of the Earth's dynamic processes.
